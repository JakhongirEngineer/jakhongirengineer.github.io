Privacy Policy

YouScore — IELTS Score Calculator
Effective date: July 6, 2026

In short: YouScore does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

YouScore ("the App") is developed by PrincipiaForge ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Data We Collect

None. YouScore has no account system, no sign-up, and no login. We do not ask for your name, email, or any other personal information, and we do not collect any usage or analytics data.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The data you enter — practice-test scores, goals, your exam date, and app settings — is stored locally on your device in an on-device database. This information never leaves your device. We cannot see it, and the App does not upload or back it up to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

None. YouScore contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics or crash-reporting SDKs, and no third-party trackers. It does not use accounts, cloud backup, or in-app purchases.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with anyone. No data is transmitted from the App to us or to any third party.

5. Network Use

YouScore works fully offline. The only time it opens an external connection is when you tap a link to another app's page, which opens the Google Play Store (or your browser). No information about you is transmitted when you do this.

6. Links to Other Apps

The App may show links to other PrincipiaForge apps (for example YouWrite and YouPlan). Tapping a link opens that app's public Google Play listing. No information about you is passed to it, and no data is shared between apps.

7. Notifications and Permissions

YouScore may ask for permission to show notifications, which power the optional study reminders (an exam countdown and a "keep your practice current" nudge). Reminders are generated on your device; no notification content is sent to any server. You can turn reminders off at any time in Settings, or deny or revoke the notification permission in your device settings.

8. Deleting Your Data

Because all data is stored only on your device, you are always in control of it. You can remove it at any time by deleting individual entries in the App, or by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App from your device settings. Uninstalling permanently removes all App data from your device.

9. Children's Privacy

YouScore does not collect data from anyone, including children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at principiaforge@gmail.com.

YouScore is an independent study aid and unofficial estimator. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to IELTS, the British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia, or Cambridge English.
