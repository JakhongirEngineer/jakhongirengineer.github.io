What is YouScore?

YouScore is a free IELTS score calculator and practice journal. Work out your overall band with the official half-band rounding rule — with the arithmetic shown so you trust the number — log every practice test, watch your trend climb toward your goal, and see exactly which skill to focus on next. Everything happens on your device, and it works fully offline.

How YouScore Uses Your Data

Local-first by design

Everything you enter — practice scores, goals, your exam date, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store your data, and nothing is uploaded.

No accounts, no tracking

YouScore has no sign-in, no advertising or analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. It works fully offline; the only network use is when you tap a link to open the Google Play Store. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

An Independent Study Aid

YouScore is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to IELTS, the British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia, or Cambridge English. "IELTS" is a trademark of its respective owners.

The band scores YouScore shows are indicative estimates to help you track your progress — not official results and not a prediction of your official IELTS score.
