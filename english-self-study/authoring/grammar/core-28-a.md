Bu darsda AJ o ªz hayotidan bir voqeani aytadi: uni ishdan bo ªshatishgan, va aynan o ªsha "muvaffaqiyatsizlik" uni bugungi erkin, mustaqil o ªqituvchiga aylantirgan. *"There is no failure, only learning."* Aynan shu g ªoyani ingliz tili bitta chiroyli qolip bilan ifodalaydi ‚Äî **aralash shart gap**: shart o ªtmishda, natija esa **hozir**. *"If I **hadn't failed**, I **wouldn't be** so strong today."* Agar o ªsha xato bo ªlmaganda, bugun bunchalik kuchli bo ªlmasdim.

### Ma ºnosi: o ªtmishdagi shart ‚Üí hozirgi natija

Oddiy (uchinchi) shart gap butunlay o ªtmishda qoladi: *If I had studied, I would have passed* ‚Äî hammasi tugagan. **Aralash shart** esa o ªtmish bilan hozirni bog ªlaydi: o ªtmishda biror narsa bo ªldi (yoki bo ªlmadi), va uning natijasi **hozir ham seziladi**. Bu ‚Äî darsning qoq markazidagi fikr: o ªtmishdagi "muvaffaqiyatsizlik" bugungi kuchingizni yasagan.

- *If I **hadn't lost** my job, I **wouldn't be** a teacher now.* ‚Äî Ishdan bo ªshamaganimda, hozir o ªqituvchi bo ªlmasdim.
- *If Watt **hadn't tried** 110 times, he **wouldn't have** his Harley today.* ‚Äî Watt 110 marta urinmaganda, bugun uning Harley ºsi bo ªlmasdi.

### Shakli: had + V3 ‚Üí would + sof fe ºl

Qolip aniq va o ªzgarmas:

- **if-qism (o ªtmish sharti):** `if + had + V3` ‚Äî *if I had failed, if she hadn't left, if we had known.*
- **asosiy qism (hozirgi natija):** `would + sof fe ºl` ‚Äî *I would be, she would understand, we would live.*

Ikki qismning zamoni **har xil** ‚Äî mana shu "aralash" degani. O ªtmish uchun `had + V3`, hozir uchun `would + fe ºl`. Ularni adashtirmang: natija `would have + V3` emas (u sof o ªtmish uchun), balki `would + fe ºl`, chunki natija hozirda.

Solishtiring:

| Qolip | if-qism | natija | ma ºnosi |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sof 3-shart | if I **had failed** | I **would have failed** | hammasi o ªtmishda |
| **Aralash** | if I **had failed** | I **would be** weak now | o ªtmish ‚Üí **hozir** |

Ikkinchi qatorga e ºtibor bering ‚Äî bu darsning g ªoyasi shu: o ªtmishdagi voqea bugungi natijani yaratgan.

### Ingliz ‚Üî o ªzbek farqi

O ªzbek tili ikkala qismni ham bir xil "-ganimda ‚Ä¶ bo ªlardim" qolipi bilan beradi va zamonlarni ajratmaydi: *"Xato qilmaganimda, hozir bilmasdim."* Ingliz tili esa shartni **o ªtmishga** (had + V3), natijani **hozirga** (would + fe ºl) qat ºiy ajratadi. Shu bois o ªzbek o ªquvchi ko ªpincha ikkala qismni ham bitta zamonda aytib yuboradi ‚Äî asosiy xato shu.

> **Eslatma:** ‚ùå *If I didn't fail, I am strong.* ‚ùå *If I hadn't failed, I wouldn't have been strong now.* ‚úÖ *If I **hadn't failed**, I **wouldn't be** strong now.* O ªtmish sharti ‚Äî `had + V3`; hozirgi natija ‚Äî `would + sof fe ºl`. Bir jumlani ovoz chiqarib takrorlang: *"If I hadn't started English, I wouldn't be here now."* Miya qolipni takrordan o ªzlashtiradi.
