### Ingliz ‚Üî o ªzbek farqi

O ªzbek tilida sabab ham, natija ham ko ªpincha bitta "-gani uchun / shuning uchun" bilan beriladi va bog ªlovchi jumla **oxirida** turadi. Ingliz tilida esa bu so ªzlarning o ªrni va uslubi farq qiladi: `because` sababni **ergashtiradi** (*because it exploded*), `so` esa natijani ikki jumla **o ªrtasida** bog ªlaydi (*it exploded, so‚Ä¶*). Eng ko ªp uchraydigan xato ‚Äî maqsadni ifodalashda `for` + fe ºl ishlatish.

Diqqat qiling: `because` va `so` **bir xil** ikki g ªoyani teskari tartibda bog ªlaydi:

- *He failed **because** he was clumsy.* (natija ‚Üí sabab)
- *He was clumsy, **so** he failed.* (sabab ‚Üí natija)

`therefore` va `as a result` esa `so`ga o ªxshaydi, ammo rasmiyroq va ko ªpincha nuqtadan keyin, vergul bilan yoziladi: *He never gave up. **Therefore**, he succeeded.*
