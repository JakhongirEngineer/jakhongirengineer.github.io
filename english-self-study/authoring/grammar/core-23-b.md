Ishtiyoq (excitement) tilda deyarli har doim **feʼl qoliplari** orqali yangraydi: *"I **love learning**, I **want to speak**, I **can't wait to start**, I'm **looking forward to** this."* AJ ham MAIN talkda kitob haqida *"I **love** this book"* deydi, POV hikoyada esa Brad *"needed money", "wanted the clerk to be patient", "started to study again"* — bularning hammasi feʼl qoliplari. Bu — gerund va infinitiv boʻyicha butun kursning yakuniy jamlanmasi.

### 1. Feʼl + -ing (gerund): love / enjoy / hate + doing

Baʼzi feʼllardan keyin ikkinchi feʼl **-ing** shaklida keladi. Bularni his qilib yodda tuting: *love, like, enjoy, hate, don't mind, finish, keep*.

- *I **love learning** English.* — Ingliz tili oʻrganishni yaxshi koʻraman.
- *I **enjoy speaking** with foreigners.* — Chet elliklar bilan gaplashishdan zavq olaman.

### 2. Feʼl + to + infinitiv: want / need / decide / hope / start + to do

Boshqa feʼllardan keyin esa **to + feʼl** keladi: *want, need, decide, hope, would like, learn, try, begin*.

- *I **want to speak** fluently.* — Ravon gapirmoqchiman.
- POV: *Brad **needed** money… he **tried to find** a way… he **started to study** again.*

*can't wait* ham shu guruhda: *I **can't wait to start**.* — Boshlashni intiqlik bilan kutyapman.

### 3. look forward to + -ing (asosiy tuzoq!)

*look forward to* — "intiqlik bilan kutmoq". Bu yerdagi **to — predlog**, infinitivning "to"si emas. Shuning uchun undan keyin **-ing** keladi, sof feʼl emas:

- *I'm **looking forward to speaking** with you.* (❗ *to speak* emas)
- *We're **looking forward to seeing** the results.*

### 4. Gerund ega sifatida: -ing jumla boshida

Feʼlni jumlaning **egasi** qilmoqchi boʻlsangiz, u **-ing** shaklga oʻtadi va yakka (birlik) hisoblanadi:

- ***Learning** English **is** exciting.* — Ingliz tili oʻrganish — hayajonli.
- ***Speaking** in public excites me.* — Ommaviy nutq meni hayajonlantiradi.

### Inglizcha ↔ oʻzbekcha farq

Oʻzbekchada *"oʻrganishni yaxshi koʻraman"* va *"gapirmoqchiman"* — ikkalasida ham **-ish / -moq**. Ingliz tili esa ikkiga boʻladi: baʼzi feʼl **-ing** talab qiladi (*love learning*), baʼzisi **to + feʼl** (*want to speak*). Qaysi feʼl qaysinisini olishini quloq bilan sezish kerak — shuning uchun POV va mini-hikoyani koʻp tinglang.

> ❌ *I want speaking. I'm looking forward to speak.* ✅ *I want to speak. I'm looking forward to speaking.* — *want* → to + feʼl; *look forward to* → -ing.

> **Eslatma:** har kuni oʻzingiz haqingizda bitta jumla ayting: *"I love learning English and I can't wait to speak it. Speaking is exciting."* Ovoz chiqarib takrorlang — qaysi feʼl qaysi qolipni olishi shu bilan avtomatlashadi.
