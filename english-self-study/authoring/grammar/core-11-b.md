"Unlimited Power" ‚Äî cheksiz imkoniyatlarni **tasavvur qilish** haqida dars. Tasavvur esa har doim bir farazdir: *"If you **had** unlimited time, what **would** you **do**?"* ‚Äî Agar cheksiz vaqtingiz **bo ªlsa edi**, nima **qilardingiz**? Marconing hikoyasi ham shundan boshlanadi: u puldor bo ªlsa-da, mukammal tanani orzu qiladi. Agar biz uning o ªrnida bo ªlsak edi, biz nima qilardik? Bu haftaning B mavzusi ‚Äî **ikkinchi shart** (second conditional): hozirgi haqiqiy bo ªlmagan, **xayoliy** vaziyatni ifodalash.

### Ma ºnosi: hozir haqiqat bo ªlmagan faraz

Ikkinchi shart **hozir** yoki **umuman** ro ªy bermaydigan, faqat tasavvurdagi holat uchun ishlatiladi. Uni yodlash oson, chunki qolipi qat ºiy:

> **If + past simple, ‚Ä¶ would + fe ºl (sof shakl).**

- *If I **had** a lot of money, I **would** travel the world.* ‚Äî Agar ko ªp pulim bo ªlsa edi, dunyo bo ªylab sayohat qilardim. (lekin pulim yo ªq)
- *If Marco **lost** everything, he **would** still be positive.* ‚Äî Marco hamma narsasini yo ªqotsa ham, baribir ijobiy bo ªlardi.
- *What **would** you **do** if you **spoke** perfect English?* ‚Äî Mukammal ingliz tilida gapirsangiz, nima qilardingiz?

Diqqat: `if` qismida o ªtgan zamon (*had, lost, spoke*) turadi, lekin ma ºnosi **o ªtmish emas** ‚Äî bu hozirgi xayoliy holat. `would` esa doim **sof fe ºl** bilan keladi: *would **travel** ‚úÖ, would to travel ‚ùå*.

### `was` emas, `were` ‚Äî barcha shaxslar uchun

Ikkinchi shartda `be` fe ºli faraz ma ºnosida odatda **were** bo ªladi, hatto *I / he / she* bilan ham:

- *If I **were** you, I **would** learn English with passion.* ‚Äî Sizning o ªrningizda bo ªlsam, ingliz tilini ehtiros bilan o ªrganardim.

*"If I **were** you‚Ä¶"* ‚Äî ingliz tilida maslahat berishning eng tabiiy yo ªli; uni tayyor ibora sifatida yodlab qo ªying.

### Birinchi shart bilan aralashtirmang

Bu ‚Äî eng ko ªp uchraydigan xato. **Haqiqiy, ehtimoli bor** kelajak uchun birinchi shart (*if + present, will*); **xayoliy, ehtimoldan uzoq** holat uchun ikkinchi shart (*if + past, would*):

- *If I **have** time tomorrow, I **will** call you.* ‚Äî real reja (vaqtim bo ªlishi mumkin).
- *If I **had** more time, I **would** learn three languages.* ‚Äî faraz (vaqtim yetmaydi).

O ªzbek tilida ikkalasi ham "agar ‚Ä¶ bo ªlsa" bilan ifodalanadi, shuning uchun o ªquvchi farqni sezmay `will` bilan farazni aytib yuboradi: ‚ùó *If I had money, I **will** buy a car.* ‚úÖ *If I had money, I **would** buy a car.*

> **Eslatma:** IELTS Part 3 da *"What would happen if‚Ä¶?"* savollari ko ªp. Har kuni bitta orzu jumlasini ovoz chiqarib ayting: *"If I **were** ‚Ä¶ , I **would** ‚Ä¶ ."* Bu qolip darajangizni darhol ko ªtaradi.
