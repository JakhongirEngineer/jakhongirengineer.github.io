Haqiqiy guruh suhbatini tinglang: *"What a tribe gives you **is** belonging. It's the people **that** keep you going. I'd say, on the whole, that's what matters."* Bu — ravonlik yakuni. Ikki qurol: **cleft** jumlalar (urgʻu berish uchun gapni ikkiga "yorib" tashlash) va **nutq bogʻlovchilari** bilan **yumshatish** (hedging). POV hikoyada Tony bir umr *yearned to make a difference* — shu maʼnoni endi urgʻu bilan aytamiz: *What Tony yearned to do was make a difference.*

### 1. What-cleft: `What … is/was …`

Oddiy gapni oling va urgʻu bermoqchi boʻlgan qismni oxiriga chiqaring, boshiga *What…* qoʻying:

- *A tribe gives you belonging.* → ***What** a tribe gives you **is** belonging.*
- *Tony wanted to help.* → ***What** Tony wanted **was** to help.* (POV — oʻtmish → *was*)
- *I love the people here.* → ***What** I love about this club **is** the people.*

Bu qolip "mana **asosiysi** shu" degan kuchli urgʻu beradi.

### 2. It-cleft: `It's … that/who …`

Bitta soʻz yoki iborani ajratib koʻrsatish uchun *It's/It was … that/who …* ishlatiladi:

- ***It's** the people **who** keep you going.* — Aynan odamlar.
- ***It was** love **that** Tony needed, not power.* — Aynan sevgi (POV — *was*).
- ***It's** respect **that** a real tribe is grounded in.*

### 3. Nutq bogʻlovchilari va yumshatish (hedging)

Ravon nutq faqat toʻgʻri grammatika emas — u **bogʻlangan** va **muloyim** boʻladi. Bu kichik iboralar fikrni ulaydi va qatʼiylikni yumshatadi:

- **Bogʻlash:** *on the whole* (umuman olganda), *to be honest* (rostini aytsam), *as I said*, *in other words*.
- **Yumshatish (hedging):** *I'd say…* (aytardimki), *kind of / sort of* (bir qadar), *in a way* (bir maʼnoda), *more or less* (taxminan).
- *"**To be honest**, a tribe is, **in a way**, a family. **I'd say** it's **more or less** about belonging."*

Yumshatish "juda qatʼiy koʻrinmaslik" uchun — IELTSʼda Fluency & Coherence ballini koʻtaradigan tabiiy belgidir.

### Inglizcha ↔ oʻzbekcha

Oʻzbek tilida urgʻuni **soʻz tartibi** yoki *"aynan"* soʻzi beradi: *"Seni **aynan mana shu odamlar** ushlab turadi"*. Ingliz tili buni maxsus qolip bilan quradi: *"**It's these people that** keep you going"*. Yumshatishda ham farq bor: oʻzbekcha *"-ku, -da, aslida"* yumshatadi; inglizchada esa *I'd say, kind of, in a way* kabi alohida iboralar bu vazifani bajaradi.

> **Eslatma:** bir oddiy jumlani oling va uni uch xil ayting — oddiy, *What…*-cleft, va *It's…that…* bilan: *"Belonging matters." / "What matters is belonging." / "It's belonging that matters."* Keyin boshiga *"To be honest, I'd say…"* qoʻshib, ovoz chiqarib takrorlang — ravonlik shundan tugʻiladi.
